Inventory Integrity Audit

A reconciliation workflow built from 20 years of floor experience — comparing system-on-hand to physical counts, logging every variance, and producing shift-level reports coordinators can act on.

Reconciliation Audit Trails Variance Reports WMS Accuracy Root-Cause Tracking

The problem

On a high-volume floor, the cost of a WMS mismatch is not abstract. A mislabel, a short shipment, or a receiving error that makes it into the system unchecked becomes a pick error, a failed audit, or a customer complaint two shifts later. This workflow addresses that gap directly.

What it does

Compare

Matches system inventory records against physical counts to surface SKUs where on-hand does not match what is actually on the shelf or in staging.

Log

Every inventory change is captured automatically — no manual audit entry, no gaps when shifts turn over or supervisors change.

Report

Produces variance reports and hot lists showing which parts generate the most repeat mismatches, by shift and zone.

Operational outcomes

  • Faster reconciliation — reduces manual count-vs-system comparison time during shift close-out
  • Repeat pattern detection — identifies SKUs and zones driving recurring discrepancies
  • Audit trail integrity — every change logged without relying on floor staff to remember to enter it
  • Supervisor-ready reports — output formatted for corrective action during handoff, not after the fact

Why this matters for logistics coordination

This is not a coding exercise. It is a demonstration of how I translate warehouse operational data into actionable reports using SQL and cloud platforms — the same skill set a Logistics Operations Coordinator needs when mislabels, shorts, and reconciliation errors are the daily reality on the floor.

Technical approach (summary)

Data matching System records joined to physical audit counts for variance detection
Integrity checks Constraints enforce valid data at entry — bad records caught before they propagate
Automated logging Changes tracked without manual intervention across shifts
Trend analysis Top mismatch SKUs ranked for targeted corrective action
